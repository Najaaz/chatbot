Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our chatbot on the Kiddoz.lk platform.

1. Information We Collect

  • Chat Inputs: We collect the messages you send during your interaction with the chatbot to provide relevant product recommendations.
  • Session Data: Basic information such as session IDs and timestamps may be stored temporarily to maintain conversation context.
  • No Account or Personal Identification: We do not request or collect personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, or payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process your product preferences and generate relevant recommendations.
  • To improve chatbot performance and user experience (in anonymised and aggregated form only).

3. Data Storage

  • Chat data is stored temporarily in session memory and not linked to any identifiable user.
  • Product recommendation outputs may be logged for debugging and improvement, but these logs do not include sensitive data.

4. Third-Party Services

Our chatbot uses OpenAI’s GPT-4o API for natural language processing. Messages sent to the chatbot may be processed by OpenAI's systems in accordance with OpenAI’s Privacy Policy.

5. Security

We follow standard security measures to protect the integrity of chatbot interactions and prevent unauthorised access. The system is hosted on a secure university server environment with restricted access.

6. Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. We recommend reviewing it regularly for any changes.

7. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the Kiddoz.lk project team via your university supervisor or academic contact.
